Who We Are

A barangay association rooted in the soil of Piapi III, united by shared labor, mutual trust, and a commitment to the people of Hamtic, Antique.

2012 Founded
Our Story

A Community
Built by Hand

We are farmers and laborers working the same land, facing the same seasons. This association exists so no one faces them alone.

Piapi III has always been a place where neighbors help neighbors — sharing tools, sharing labor, and looking out for one another through good harvests and hard ones.

We formed this association to make that support official. To give it structure, a name, and a place at the table when decisions are made.

🤝

Unity

We work better together than apart. What one member carries, the group helps shoulder.

⚖️

Fairness

Decisions are made with all members in mind. Every voice in the association has weight.

🌱

Sustainability

We take care of the land because the land takes care of us. What we plant today, our children will harvest.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

The principles that shape every decision we make and every program we run.

🌾

Stewardship of the Land

The land is not just a resource — it is what connects us. We treat it with care and pass it on in better shape than we found it.

01
🤲

Mutual Aid

When one member struggles, the rest take notice. Support flows both ways — no one is expected to give without also being able to receive.

02
📋

Transparency

Members have a right to know how the association is run, how resources are used, and what decisions are being made on their behalf.

03
🏘️

Community First

Every program, every decision, every effort begins with the same question: does this serve the people of Piapi III?

04
📚

Continuous Learning

Better farming, better governance, better lives — these come from staying open to new knowledge and sharing it freely among members.

05
⚖️

Equity & Inclusion

The association is for all farmers and laborers in the barangay, regardless of the size of their land or the scale of their work.

Our Journey

A Brief History

Key milestones in the life of the association.

2012

Association Founded

Farmers and laborers of Piapi III came together to formally establish the association — putting structure behind what the community had always done informally.

2014

First Farm Input Program

The association coordinated its first collective access to seeds and materials, making resources available to members at the start of the planting season.

2018

LGU Partnership Established

A working relationship with the local government unit was formalized, opening doors to training programs, assistance, and wider recognition of the association.

2023

Skills Training Program Launch

Members began participating in DA-assisted training sessions, bringing new knowledge in crop management and sustainable practices back to the barangay.

2025

Digital Records & New Officers

A new set of officers was elected by the membership, carrying the association forward with fresh energy and a commitment to transparency and growth.
