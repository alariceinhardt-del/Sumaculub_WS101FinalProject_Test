Why Join

Membership
Benefits

Membership in the Piapi III FLA is open to all farmers and laborers residing in the barangay. There are no fees to join — only a willingness to participate and contribute to the collective good of the community.

  • 🌱

    Farm Input Support

    Access to seeds, tools, and farming materials coordinated through association funds.

  • 💰

    Financial Assistance

    Emergency aid and member loans for hardship, crop loss, or unexpected family needs.

  • 📚

    Skills & Training

    Workshops on agricultural techniques, cooperative governance, and financial literacy.

  • 🤝

    Labor Exchange

    Member-to-member labor sharing during peak seasons to reduce hiring costs.

  • ⚖️

    Equal Vote & Voice

    Every member has a vote in every decision made by the association.

Requirements

  • Resident of Barangay Piapi III, Hamtic, Antique
  • Active farmer or laborer in the barangay
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Barangay clearance or certificate of residency
  • Hard copy of land title or tax declaration
