How to Access a Program

The process is straightforward. Members in good standing may apply at any time.

1

Verify Membership

Confirm your standing with the Association Secretary prior to application.

2

Submit Request

Approach an officer or submit a written request at the Barangay Hall.

3

Review

The officers review all requests and notify applicants of the outcome.

4

Receive Assistance

Approved members receive assistance per the program's terms and schedule.

Ready to Participate?

Become a Member

All programs are open exclusively to registered members of the Association. Membership is free and open to all farmers and laborers of Barangay Piapi III.
