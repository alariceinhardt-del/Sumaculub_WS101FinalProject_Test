Who We Are

A Community
Built by Hand

We are farmers and laborers working the same land, facing the same seasons. This association exists so no one faces them alone.

Piapi III has always been a place where neighbors help neighbors — sharing tools, sharing labor, and looking out for one another through good harvests and hard ones.

We formed this association to make that support official. To give it structure, a name, and a place at the table when decisions are made.

Our Programs

Practical support built around the real needs of our farmers, laborers, and their families.

🌱

Farm Input Support

Seeds, tools, and materials are easier to access as a group. The association works to connect members with input resources when the planting season comes.

01
💰

Financial Assistance

Members facing unexpected hardship can seek support through the association. We look at what we can do together before anyone is left to manage alone.

02
📚

Seed Distribution

Quality seeds are the start of every harvest. The association facilitates access to seeds for members, coordinated through available government programs and shared resources.

03
🤝

Labor Exchange

Planting and harvest are heavy work. Members help each other in the field — time given is time returned when your own season comes.

04
🌾

Market Linkage

We look for ways to connect members' produce to buyers and markets beyond the barangay, so the work in the field is not wasted at the gate.

05
🏘️

Welfare & Community

Beyond farming, members are people with families. The association takes note of those who need a hand and responds the way neighbors do.
